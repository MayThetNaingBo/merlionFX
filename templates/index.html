Forex in General

Forex (foreign exchange) is the global market for trading currencies. It is the world's largest financial market, with over $6 trillion traded daily. Unlike traditional stock markets, the forex market operates 24 hours a day, five days a week, and spans across major financial centers like London, New York, Tokyo, and Sydney.
